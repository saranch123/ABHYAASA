# ABHYAASA (अभ्यास)

**ABHYAASA** is a **confidence training platform** for students.

It helps students overcome inferiority and performance anxiety by **training confidence as a skill**, not by motivation, therapy, or affirmations.

The platform places users in **realistic academic, interview, and social scenarios**, where they practice under **controlled pressure** using AI-guided sessions. Confidence is built through **repeated exposure, measurable feedback, and progressive difficulty**, not emotional reassurance.

ABHYAASA is **not** a mental health or wellness product.  
It does **not** provide therapy, diagnosis, or emotional counselling.

The system focuses on:
- Exposure-based practice  
- Adaptive role-play with awkward situations  
- Perspective-switch replay to understand intent and reduce misinterpretation  
- Objective scoring and skill progression  

Confidence in ABHYAASA is earned through practice.
